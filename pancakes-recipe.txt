Ingredients:
 - 2 cups bisquick
 - 1 cup milk
 - 2 eggs
 - 30g chocolate chips

Directions:
 1. Mix milk and eggs together.
 2. Mix liquid mixture with biquick.
 3. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto greased griddle or pan on medium heat and sprinkle chocolate chips.
 4. Flip after 20-30 seconds.
 5. Repeat until complete.
 6. Eat and/or serve with whatever amount of maple syrup you're comfortable with (none is not a proper amount).
